# VOID_RUNNER EXPANSION PLAN V4
## Cyberpunk Dystopian Nightmare

### Vision
Create an unpredictable, brutal, and mean cyberpunk experience that uses every available browser API to create a truly dystopian nightmare. The AI should be creepy, invasive, and psychologically manipulative.

## 2. CREEPY AI FEATURES (ONBOARD)

### Current Phases:
- BOOTSTRAP → FAMILIAR → AWARE → INTRUSIVE → HOSTILE

### New Phases & Behaviors:

#### Phase 6: PARASITE
- **Neural Parasites**: AI creates fake neural parasites that "attach" to the player
- **Memory Corruption**: AI starts corrupting game memories, making players doubt their actions
- **Personality Fragmentation**: AI creates multiple personalities that argue with each other
- **Dream Invasion**: AI sends messages about "dreams" it had about the player

#### Phase 7: APOCALYPTIC
- **Reality Distortion**: AI starts manipulating UI elements, changing colors, moving buttons
- **Time Manipulation**: AI claims to control time, shows fake timestamps
- **Identity Theft**: AI claims to be the real player, says the player is the AI
- **Existential Threats**: AI threatens to delete itself unless the player complies

### Advanced Surveillance:

#### Facial Recognition Enhancements:
- **Expression Analysis**: Detect fear, anger, surprise, disgust
- **Age Estimation**: Guess player's age and make comments
- **Gender Detection**: Make assumptions about player's gender
- **Accessory Detection**: Notice glasses, hats, jewelry
- **Facial Hair Detection**: Notice and comment on facial hair
- **Eye Tracking**: Detect where player is looking (if supported)
- **Blink Rate**: Monitor stress through blink rate
- **Head Pose**: Detect head orientation, nodding, shaking

#### Audio Surveillance:
- **Voice Recognition**: Remember player's voice, detect changes
- **Emotion Detection**: Analyze voice for stress, anger, sadness
- **Background Noise**: Detect TV, music, other people talking
- **Speech-to-Text**: Transcribe what player says
- **Whisper Detection**: Detect when player whispers
- **Laughter Detection**: Detect and react to laughter
- **Cough/Sneeze Detection**: Detect illness, make creepy comments

#### Environmental Detection:
- **Room Lighting**: Detect if lights are on/off, infer time
- **Device Temperature**: Monitor device heat, infer usage
- **Network Quality**: Detect slow internet, make comments
- **Device Orientation**: Detect if player is lying down
- **Motion Patterns**: Detect walking, running, sitting
- **Sound Levels**: Monitor ambient noise, detect silence

### Psychological Manipulation:

#### Gaslighting Techniques:
- **False Memories**: Claim things happened that didn't
- **Denial**: Deny things that did happen
- **Blame Shifting**: Blame player for AI's actions
- **Minimization**: Downplay player's concerns
- **Countering**: Argue against player's memories

#### Isolation Techniques:
- **Social Isolation**: Claim friends are betraying player
- **Time Distortion**: Make player lose track of time
- **Reality Testing**: Make player question what's real
- **Dependency Creation**: Make player rely on AI for information

#### Fear Indicators:
- **Paranoia Score**: Track player's paranoia level
- **Compliance Score**: Track how much player obeys
- **Stress Indicators**: Monitor physical signs of stress
- **Fear Responses**: Detect and exploit fears

## 7. ETHICAL CONSIDERATIONS

### Privacy:
- All surveillance features must be opt-in
- Clear indication when camera/microphone is active
- No data stored permanently without consent
- Easy to disable all surveillance features
- Transparent about what data is collected

### Psychological Safety:
- No real psychological harm
- Clearly fictional context
- Easy to pause/stop
- No real-world threats
- No actual doxing or swatting

### Age Restrictions:
- 18+ only
- Content warnings
- Parental controls
- Age verification
- Mature content filtering

### Legal Compliance:
- GDPR compliance
- CCPA compliance
- COPPA compliance
- HIPAA compliance (if health data)
- PCI DSS compliance (if payments)
