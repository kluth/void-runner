# VOID_RUN MANUAL: BOTNETS, DDOS & RANSOMWARE

## 14.1 THE SCALE OF BRUTALITY
In the claustrophobic neon-grid of VOID_RUN, power is not merely measured by the clock speed of your deck or the sophistication of your bypass algorithms. It is measured by volume. To dismantle the high-tier corporate monoliths and pierce the Aegis-class firewalls of the Inner Core, a single runner is often insufficient. You must weaponize the digital landscape itself. You must turn the very infrastructure of the connected world into a bludgeon. This is the essence of mass-scale disruption: the transition from surgical intrusion to total digital warfare.

## 14.2 BOTNETS: THE SILENT HIVE
A Botnet is a decentralized network of compromised "Zombie" nodes—ranging from high-end corporate servers to mundane IoT appliances—all tethered to your central Command & Control (C2) node. In the VOID_RUN universe, these are the "Ghost Legions."

### Infection and Propagation
*   **Worm-Logic:** Deploy self-replicating scripts that exploit unpatched vulnerabilities in common OS kernels. These worms crawl through the mesh, infecting every susceptible node they encounter.
*   **Social Engineering Payloads:** Intercept encrypted mail-streams and inject trojans disguised as high-priority corporate memos or security patches.
*   **Supply Chain Subversion:** Compromise a low-level library in a common software update, allowing your code to be signed and distributed by trusted vendors.

### Maintenance and Attrition
A Botnet is a living, dying thing. Nodes are constantly lost to security wipes, system patches, or physical hardware failures. A successful Runner must engage in continuous "re-seeding" to maintain the hive's size. Balancing the size of your network against its visibility is a constant tension; a million-node botnet is a god-tier weapon, but its traffic patterns are unmistakable to the System Integrity Watchdogs.

## 14.3 DDOS: KINETIC PRESSURE IN SILICON
Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) is the digital equivalent of a planetary siege. By commanding your Ghost Legion to flood a target IP with a relentless torrent of junk data, you saturate their bandwidth and exhaust their CPU cycles until the system reaches a state of "Critical Hang."

*   **Amplification Vectors:** Exploit protocols like DNS, NTP, or Memcached to turn a whisper of a request into a scream of a response, magnifying your Botnet's output by orders of magnitude.
*   **Protocol Shifting:** Advanced targets employ adaptive hardware firewalls. You must dynamically rotate your attack patterns—shifting between UDP floods, TCP-SYN exhaustion, and application-layer HTTP GET saturation—to bypass their filters.
*   **Strategic Diversion:** Often, the DDoS is not the goal. It is the smoke screen. While the target's security team is frantically trying to mitigate a 40-Terabit flood, you can slip through an overlooked port to perform a quiet, surgical data-heist.

## 14.4 RANSOMWARE: THE FINAL EXTORTION
Ransomware represents the most brutal application of cryptography. It is the process of seizing a target's most valuable asset—their data—and placing it behind an unbreakable lock.

### The Lifecycle of a Strike
1.  **Exfiltration (The Silent Theft):** Never encrypt before you steal. The modern Runner exfiltrates sensitive data first, ensuring that even if the target restores from backups, you still have leverage.
2.  **Deployment of VOID_CRYPT:** Once the data is secured, trigger the payload. Our proprietary `VOID_CRYPT` algorithm uses 4096-bit asymmetric encryption. Without the private key residing on your C2 node, the data is mathematically indistinguishable from noise.
3.  **The Countdown:** A ransom note is injected into every display interface on the target network. In the brutalist economy of VOID_RUN, time is the ultimate currency. If the Credits are not transferred within the shrinking window, the C2 server automatically purges the decryption key, rendering the target's data permanently inaccessible.
