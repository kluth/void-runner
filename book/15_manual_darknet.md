# MANUAL: DARKNET NODES & SYNDICATES

## 1.0 THE UNDER-GRID
In the oppressive landscape of VOID_RUN, the surface-level web is a sterilized facade maintained by corporate hegemonies. The true architecture of the digital world lies within the **Darknet Nodes**—non-Euclidean data clusters that exist in the interstitial gaps between global firewalls. These nodes are not hosted on central servers; they are parasitic entities, feeding on the wasted cycles of industrial infrastructure and forgotten satellite arrays. To enter a Node is to step outside the protection of the law and into a realm of raw, unencrypted reality.

### 1.1 Node Classifications
*   **Ghost Relays:** Ephemeral entry points that utilize heartbeat-hopping to avoid triangulation. They are the lifeblood of the independent runner, providing quick data-drop capabilities before they self-terminate upon detection.
*   **Brutalist Hubs:** Heavily fortified data fortresses maintained by the Syndicates. These are monuments to digital excess, featuring layers of active IC (Intrusion Countermeasures) and physical air-gaps. They house the most lucrative exploits and forbidden archives.
*   **Deep-Sinks:** Unmapped, lawless territories where the code has begun to decay. These are remnants of failed experiments or collapsed networks. Enter at your own risk; the logic here is predatory.

## 2.0 THE SYNDICATES: ARCHITECTS OF THE VOID
The Darknet is not a vacuum; it is a battlefield governed by Syndicates—loose confederations of elite runners, rogue AI consciousnesses, and industrial saboteurs. To survive the VOID, you must learn to navigate their conflicting agendas.

### 2.1 The Concrete Maw
**Philosophy:** Absolute Efficiency.
The Maw controls the primary hardware black markets. They operate with the cold precision of an automated assembly line. They value runners who can execute clean, silent breaches without leaving a trace. To the Maw, ego is a bug; performance is the only metric.
*   **Asset Access:** Overclocked neural bridges and high-density cryogenic storage.
*   **Penalty:** The Maw does not forgive incompetence. A failed mission results in your biometric data being leaked to SEC-COP databases as a "gift."

### 2.2 Silicon Shadows
**Philosophy:** Information is the Ultimate Currency.
A technocratic cult obsessed with the "First Code"—the theoretical foundation of the global grid. They specialize in deep-tier decryption and the recovery of "dark data" from the pre-Collapse era. They deal in secrets rather than credits.
*   **Asset Access:** Advanced algorithmic decryption suites and signal-ghosting modules.
*   **Penalty:** If you cross the Shadows, your digital identity won't just be deleted—it will be rewritten. You may find your bank accounts belonging to a dead man and your citizenship revoked.

### 2.3 Iron Lung
**Philosophy:** Total System Destabilization.
Anarchist-accelerationists who believe the current world must be burnt down to make way for the new. Their missions are loud, destructive, and often involve massive collateral damage to corporate infrastructure. 
*   **Asset Access:** Heavy-duty malware payloads, logic bombs, and system-crashing "Sledgehammer" protocols.
*   **Penalty:** Working with Iron Lung guarantees a Maximum Threat Level (MTL). You will be hunted by every corporate hit-squad on the planet.

## 3.0 SYN-VAL (SYNDICATE VALUE)
Your standing within the Darknet is tracked via **SYN-VAL**. This is a weighted reputation system that dictates your level of access to forbidden tools and high-tier contracts.
*   **Noise (0-20%):** You are a peripheral nuisance. Transaction fees are astronomical.
*   **Asset (21-60%):** You have proven your utility. Basic Syndicate-specific gear is unlocked.
*   **Ghost (61-90%):** You are a trusted operative. You are invited to high-stakes raids, and your signal is actively masked by Syndicate relays.
*   **Architect (91-100%):** You are the one who issues the contracts. You influence Syndicate policy and hold the keys to the kingdom.

**WARNING:** In the VOID, loyalty is a resource, and like any resource, it can be depleted. Choose your masters wisely.
