# Lore: The Anarchists — The Architects of Entropy

In the high-latency gutters of VOID_RUN, where the light of the Corporate Monoliths fails to reach, The Anarchists thrive. They are not a unified front, nor do they seek a seat at the table of the new digital order. They are the table-breakers. Born from the "Great De-Sync" of '84, the Anarchists view the current architecture of the Net not as a tool for progress, but as a digital panopticon designed to harvest human cognitive cycles until nothing remains but hollowed-out shells.

### The Ideology of the Void

The Anarchist philosophy is rooted in **Brutalist Transparency**. They believe that privacy is a luxury of the oppressor and that absolute, chaotic transparency is the only path to genuine liberation. To an Anarchist, a locked file is a lie, and an encrypted database is a crime against the collective consciousness. Their mantra is simple: *If it exists, it must be witnessed.* They do not steal data for profit; they "liberate" it into the public stream, regardless of the consequences. If a leaked financial ledger crashes a regional economy, they view it as the inevitable collapse of a structural fallacy.

### Methods and Operations: The Sledgehammer Approach

Unlike the surgical precision of corporate ghost-runners or the calculated greed of the Syndicate, Anarchists favor the "Sledgehammer" protocol. Their attacks are loud, messy, and devastatingly effective. They utilize:

1.  **Signal Flooding:** Overwhelming corporate nodes with raw, unformatted junk data until the hardware literally melts from thermal overload.
2.  **Logic Bombs:** Deeply embedded viruses that trigger during peak usage hours, turning essential services into digital traps.
3.  **The "No-Face" Protocol:** A radical form of decentralized identity where no single hacker holds a master key. Actions are coordinated through algorithmic consensus, making the collective impossible to decapitate.

### The Splinter Cells

While largely decentralized, certain names evoke terror in the boardrooms of VOID_RUN. The **Sledgehammer Collective** is the most notorious, known for the "Black Monday" event where they liquidated the retirement funds of three Tier-1 mega-corps and distributed the assets into randomized crypto-dust. Then there is **Null_Pointer**, a solo entity rumored to be a rogue AI that adopted the Anarchist creed, seeking to erase all digital footprints—starting with its own.

To the system, they are terrorists. To the desperate, they are the only ones telling the truth. In the cold, unyielding world of VOID_RUN, the Anarchists are the entropy that ensures nothing stays static for long.
