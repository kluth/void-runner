# VOID_RUN OPERATOR MANUAL | SECTION 22: PHISHING & SOCIAL ENGINEERING

## THE HUMAN VULNERABILITY
In the cold, geometric reality of the VOID, code is absolute. However, the machines we target are still maintained, operated, and guarded by carbon-based entities. While a firewall is a mathematical barrier, a human mind is a chaotic mess of biases, fears, and misplaced trust. Social Engineering is the art of exploiting these cognitive glitches to bypass the most sophisticated encryption. In VOID_RUN, mastering the "meatware" is often faster than brute-forcing a mainframe.

## PHISHING TACTICS
Phishing is your primary scalpel for extracting credentials without triggering a single IDS alert. You aren't just sending emails; you are crafting a false reality.

### 1. SPEAR-PHISHING (THE TARGETED STRIKE)
Unlike broad sweeps, spear-phishing requires reconnaissance. Use the `NMAP` and `WHOIS` tools to identify specific employees within a corporate hierarchy. A spoofed memo from the CTO regarding "Mandatory Security Updates" has a 75% higher success rate than a generic "Account Locked" notification. 
*   **Gameplay Tip:** Ensure your `SPOOF_HEADER` matches the target's internal domain exactly. One typo in the sender address and the sysadmin will be on you in seconds.

### 2. PRETEXTING
This involves creating a fabricated scenario—a pretext—to steal personal information. In VOID_RUN, this often manifests as the "Support Desk" gambit. You initiate a chat or VoIP call to a low-level clerk, claiming to be from Central IT. 
*   **The Hook:** "We've detected an unauthorized login from your terminal. I need your temporary token to reset the node." 
*   **The Payoff:** If successful, they will hand over their `AUTH_TOKEN`, granting you high-level access without a single exploit package.

## ADVANCED TECHNIQUES: BAITING & QUID PRO QUO
Sometimes, greed is more effective than fear.
*   **Baiting:** Leaving "infected" data packets in public nodes or file-sharing sectors. A file named `Q4_BONUS_STRUCTURE.PDF.EXE` is irresistible to the corporate drones. Once they "collect" it, your backdoor is installed.
*   **Quid Pro Quo:** Offering a "service" in exchange for information. "I can fix your laggy connection if you give me the gateway IP." It’s simple, brutal, and effective.

## THE GOLDEN RULE OF THE VOID
Never forget: A system is only as secure as the person holding the key. If you can convince them to open the door for you, you don't need to pick the lock. But beware—social engineering leaves a trail of witnesses. Meatware remembers faces and voices. Move fast, extract the data, and vanish back into the static.
