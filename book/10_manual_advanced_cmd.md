# Manual: Advanced Terminal Commands

## 0x01. The Neural Interface: Beyond the Prompt
The VOID_OS terminal is not merely a command-line interface; it is a direct neural uplink to the global data grid. While a novice can execute a simple `ls` or `status` check, a VOID_RUNNER must master the high-level protocols that define the edge between extraction and incineration. These advanced commands are your primary tools for surviving the brutalist digital landscape where the "Blue Team"—the corporate-funded hunters—monitors every byte of entropy. Each command executed is a ripple in the digital ether; mastering the magnitude of these ripples is the difference between a successful ghost-run and a physical SWAT raid at your meat-space location.

## 0x02. Thermal & Logic Management
High-intensity operations generate thermal waste and logic corruption. Ignoring these metrics will result in a `KERNEL_PANIC`, memory leaks, or permanent physical hardware lockout.

- **`status`**: The most critical diagnostic. It returns your `SYSTEM_HEALTH`, `TRACE_LEVEL`, and `SYSTEM_HEAT`. Monitor this during every breach. If health drops below 30%, data decay begins, and your credits will slowly leak into the void.
- **`cooldown`**: When your neural rig hits the thermal ceiling, the `cooldown` protocol injects liquid nitrogen into your hardware slots, reducing `SYSTEM_HEAT` by 30%. Without this, your modules will face logic-bomb debuffs and permanent data decay.
- **`abort_purge [CODE]`**: If your detection level reaches 100%, the grid initiates a `PURGE_SEQUENCE`. You have 30 seconds to enter the randomized, hexadecimal override code displayed on your screen. Failure results in a total system wipe—credits, reputation, and active missions are incinerated instantly.

## 0x03. Stealth & Signal Obfuscation
The grid is a hostile, surveillance-heavy environment. Silence is your only true armor, and obfuscation is your only shield.

- **`wipe`**: The "Ghost Protocol." Requires the `log-wiper` utility. This command incinerates your active log trail, resetting your `TRACE_LEVEL` to zero. It is the ultimate tool for vanishing before the Blue Team locks your location.
- **`routing [DIRECT|VPN|ONION]`**: Configure your traffic flow through various anonymization layers. While `ONION` routing carries a heavy credit cost and higher latency, it provides a 3.0x stealth multiplier, essential for high-tier corporate breaches and long-term surveillance.
- **`neural_dive --deep`**: A high-risk, high-reward protocol that bypasses standard neural safety limiters. It drastically reduces your `TRACE_LEVEL` by shifting your signal into high-frequency, "void" bands. **Warning:** This causes severe visual distortion and sensory overload (hallucinations) for up to 10 seconds.

## 0x04. Advanced Utility & Counter-Hacking
- **`clear`**: Purges the local terminal logs from your display buffer. This does not affect your trace level on the grid but clears your mental overhead during chaotic intrusion events.
- **`ls -a`**: When inside a compromised server or internal network, use this to reveal hidden system artifacts, encrypted firmware blocks, and zero-day fragments.
- **`signal_jam`**: (Requires Signal Jammer hardware) Delays incoming Blue Team retaliation alerts by 30 seconds, providing a critical window for final data exfiltration or a desperate `wipe` command.

In VOID_RUN, the terminal is your weapon, your shield, and your eventual coffin. Use it with lethal precision, or let the void claim what remains of your identity.
