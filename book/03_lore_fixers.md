# Lore: The Fixers — Shadows of the VOID

In the crushing architecture of the VOID, where data is as heavy as lead and failure is permanent, you don't survive by skill alone. You survive because someone allows you to. These gatekeepers are known as The Fixers—the lubricant that keeps the rusted gears of the digital underground turning. They are neither heroes nor villains; they are the essential infrastructure of the brutalist nightmare.

## The Necessary Evil
The Fixers operate in the gray spaces between the monolithic corporate nodes and the desperate sprawl of the unlisted sectors. While the corporations own the hardware and the runners possess the talent, Fixers own the *connections*. They are the high-frequency traders of human capital, matching high-risk contracts with runners desperate enough to take them. In VOID_RUN, a Fixer is your first point of contact, your quartermaster, and often, your only link to a world outside the terminal.

## Brutalist Arbitrators
In keeping with the brutalist ethos of the VOID, Fixers do not deal in pleasantries. Their communication is sparse, functional, and devoid of empathy. They view a runner as a disposable asset—a packet of data to be transmitted through a firewall. If the packet arrives, the Fixer takes their cut. If it’s lost, the Fixer simply routes around the failure. To a Fixer, the concrete walls of the physical world and the rigid logic of the digital one are the same: obstacles to be bypassed through the application of pressure and credits.

## Services and Scarcity
A Fixer provides what the VOID denies: context. They offer:
- **Sanitized Contracts:** Jobs stripped of corporate identifiers to provide a thin layer of plausible deniability.
- **Black-Market Hardware:** Overclocked rigs and illegal signal boosters that haven't been logged by the central registries.
- **Information Brokerage:** The location of a hidden back-door or the shift schedule of a particular sysadmin.

But these services come at a cost that transcends the digital. Dealing with a Fixer means entering a debt cycle that is almost impossible to break. Every favor is a leash, and every piece of gear is a tracker.

## The Code of the Gray
While they lack traditional morals, Fixers adhere to a strict protocol of transactional integrity. A Fixer who burns their runners loses their source of income; a runner who burns their Fixer loses their life. In the VOID, reputation is the only currency that isn't subject to inflation. When you see a notification from your Fixer, remember: they aren't your friend. They are the mirror reflecting your own survival back at you, one brutal contract at a time.
