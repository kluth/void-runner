# Lore: Legendary Hackers

In the crushing concrete labyrinth of VOID_RUN, history is not written by the victors, but by those who remained unseen while the foundations of the old world crumbled. These are the ghosts in the machine—the legendary hackers whose exploits are whispered in encrypted channels and etched into the silicon memory of the deep web.

## Zero-Day-Zero
Before the VOID was a battleground, it was a silent frontier. Zero-Day-Zero is the progenitor, the mythic figure who allegedly discovered the "Primal Vulnerability" in the Global Central Kernel. Legend has it that Zero-Day-Zero didn't just find a bug; they found a way to treat the entire digital world as a guest process. Their final act was the "Transcendence Protocol," a massive data-dump that decentralized the world's wealth in a single millisecond before their physical node was vaporized by a kinetic strike. No body was ever found, leading many to believe they uploaded their consciousness directly into the buffer.

## The Architect
If Zero-Day-Zero found the hole, The Architect built the fortress around it. Known for a brutalist approach to code, The Architect’s scripts are legendary for their terrifying efficiency and lack of "human" elements like comments or variable names. They designed the first iterations of the VOID's ice-walls, creating structures so complex that attempting to decrypt them often results in hardware meltdowns. The Architect disappeared during the "Great Reconstruction," but their influence remains in the monolith-like structures that dominate the deep-VOID layers.

## Circuit Breaker
Not all legends are builders. Circuit Breaker was a force of pure entropy. During the era of the Megacorp Hegemony, Circuit Breaker orchestrated the "Blackout of '92," a coordinated attack that physically destroyed server farms by manipulating power grids to create harmonic resonances. They believed that digital tyranny could only be solved by physical destruction. Circuit Breaker was eventually caught after a three-day siege of their bunker, but even in custody, they managed to wipe the arresting agency's entire criminal database using a hidden transmitter in their dental implants.

## Glitch-Witch
A modern legend, Glitch-Witch treats the VOID not as a machine, but as an ecosystem. They are famous for "data-weaving"—a technique that uses intentional race conditions and memory leaks to create temporary, "impossible" states in the system. To watch Glitch-Witch work is to see the screen dissolve into a beautiful, terrifying mosaic of errors that somehow execute perfect logic. They represent the new wave of hackers: those who don't just break the rules, but rewrite the physics of the digital realm.

These legends serve as the North Star for every runner. In the VOID, you either become a ghost, or you become a warning.
