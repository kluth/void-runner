# Lore: The Mega-Corporations

In the grey, monolithic world of **VOID_RUN**, the concept of the nation-state has long since crumbled under the weight of hyper-efficient profit extraction. What remains are the "Slabs"—the four Mega-Corporations that divided the planet into industrial sectors, digital domains, and neural zones. They don't just own the land; they own the bandwidth of human thought and the architecture of existence itself.

### AETHEL-KOLOSS: The Concrete Architects
Aethel-Koloss is the progenitor of the "Brutalist Renaissance." They believe that architecture should reflect absolute power—imposing, unyielding, and impossible to ignore. Their corporate headquarters are windowless, miles-high monoliths of reinforced polymer-concrete that cast permanent shadows over the lower tiers of the city. In the digital realm, their servers are structured like fortresses—linear, massive, and incredibly difficult to navigate without high-level clearance. They control the global power grid and the physical fiber-optic arteries that connect the dying megacities.

### NEURAL-THREAT SYSTEMS (NTS): The Executioners
If you encounter "Black ICE" (Intrusion Countermeasures Electronics) that can fry your synapses in milliseconds, it likely carries the NTS watermark. Originally a private military contractor, NTS pivoted to digital security when the wars moved from the physical to the data-stream. They view hackers—or "Runners"—not as criminals, but as "systemic anomalies" to be purged. Their security daemons are modeled after predatory instincts, hunting through the VOID with relentless efficiency. Their motto: *Elimination is the only Patch.*

### VOID-DATA CONSOLIDATED (VDC): The Information Monolith
VDC owns the protocols. Every packet of data sent across the globe passes through a VDC gateway. They have achieved the ultimate corporate dream: the absolute commodification of silence. If you aren't paying VDC for "Priority Transmission," your data is slowed to a crawl, or "lost" in the buffers. They maintain the "Great Filter," an algorithmic censorship machine that ensures the populace only sees what is profitable for the Slabs. To VDC, truth is a variable that can be edited in real-time.

### SYNTAX-MINING CORP: The Soul Harvesters
The most insidious of the four, Syntax-Mining specializes in "Human Asset Extraction." They provide the neural links used by the masses to escape the grey reality for the neon simulations of the Upper Tiers. In exchange, they "mine" the subconscious of their users, harvesting raw creative thought and emotional responses to train their next generation of AI-overlords. To Syntax-Mining, a human brain is just another processor to be overclocked until it burns out.

To a **VOID_RUNNER**, these corporations are not just enemies; they are the environment itself. You don't "defeat" them; you survive them. You slip through the cracks of their monolithic code, steal what you can, and pray your deck is fast enough to outrun their reach.
