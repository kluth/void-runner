# Guide: Quantum Breaches

## Overview
In the brutalist digital landscape of VOID_RUN, standard cryptographic attacks are mere child's play. When you encounter a **Quantum-Locked Node**, you are no longer just crunching numbers; you are manipulating the fundamental fabric of information. Quantum Breaches represent the pinnacle of high-stakes hacking, where a single misstep doesn't just result in a "Connection Terminated" message—it can physically incinerate your neural interface and leave your terminal a melted husk of silicon and regret.

## The Nature of the Beast
Quantum encryption in VOID_RUN relies on the volatile principle of **Superposition**. Unlike a classical bit, which is restricted to 1 or 0, a Q-Bit exists in both states simultaneously until observed. The security architecture of Tier-1 megacorps (AETHER-CORP, NEURAL-SYNC, and the dreaded VOID-FOUNDATION) utilizes **Entangled Sentinel Chains**. These chains are not just locks; they are living mathematical entities. If you attempt to "observe" or probe the data without the proper phase-alignment, the chain suffers a catastrophic collapse, triggering a total data wipe and a lethal feedback loop back to the intruder's deck.

## Mechanics of the Breach
To successfully navigate a Quantum Breach, a Void-Runner must master three distinct, high-pressure phases:

1.  **Phase Synchronization:** You must align your terminal’s internal clock with the target node’s oscillation frequency. This requires manual throttle control of your cooling systems to maintain a stable environment. If your sync rate drops below 98.4%, your packets suffer from **Decoherence**, manifesting as visual static and alerting the system's active ICE.
2.  **Entanglement Mapping:** This is the core puzzle. You must identify which Q-Bits are linked across the node's architecture. Altering the state of a "Leader" bit will inevitably flip its "Shadow" twin. You must resolve these pairings in real-time while the node's entropy clock accelerates toward zero.
3.  **The Final Observation:** Once the mapping is verified, you must initiate the 'Wavefunction Collapse'. This is the moment of truth where you force the system into a singular, decrypted state. Failure here is usually fatal.

## The Risk: Neural Scramble
The hardware required for Quantum Breaching is notoriously unstable. An unsuccessful breach often results in **Thermal Feedback**. In the world of VOID_RUN, this means your deck's cooling fans fail, and the sudden surge of raw, unshielded data leads to permanent **Synaptic Scarring**. Symptoms include phantom terminal sounds, visual matrix-glitches in your real-world vision, and in extreme cases, a total cerebral flatline.

## Pro-Tips for Void-Runners
*   **Cryo-Pumping:** Ensure your Rig’s liquid nitrogen levels are at 100% before attempting a Level 5 Breach. Heat is the enemy of coherence.
*   **Pattern Recognition:** Quantum states often mirror the runner's own neural rhythms. If the Entanglement Map looks chaotic, breathe and wait for the harmonic resonance.
*   **Echo-Buffering:** Always deploy a sacrificial 'Ghost Deck' to absorb the initial decoherence shock. It is an expensive insurance policy, but far cheaper than a brain transplant.
