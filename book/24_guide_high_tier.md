# GUIDE: HIGH-TIER MISSIONS // [BLACK-TIER OPERATIONS]

## Overview: The Meat Grinder
High-Tier missions, colloquially known as "Black-Tier" or "Void-Walker" contracts, are the apex of a Runner's career. These are not your standard script-kiddie database scrapes. We are talking about infiltrating hardened Syndicate bastions, high-frequency trade-jacking, and orbital satellite hijacking. At this level, the Grid Architect stops being a fair referee and starts being a predator. The margin for error is non-existent; a single mistyped hex address can lead to total system failure.

## 1. Threshold Requirements
To even see these contracts on your `ls` output, an operative typically requires:
- **Reputation (REP):** 5,000+.
- **Neural Power (NW):** A minimum of 80 NW available for high-latency modules.
- **Hardware Synergy:** You cannot rely on a single WiFi Pineapple. You need a modular rig combining the **Darknet Router** for trace suppression and the **Quantum Decryptor** for bypassing 256-bit entropy walls.

## 2. The Adversary: Corporate Black ICE
Standard blue teams are replaced by automated "Black ICE" (Intrusion Countermeasure Electronics). If your Trace hits 100% during a High-Tier breach, the retaliation is not just a lockout—it is a **Neural Feedback Loop**. Expect extreme visual distortion, kernel logic bombs that scramble your command history, and "The Void's Eye" which will demand immediate neural synchronization via complex riddles. Failure to solve these in under 30 seconds often results in permanent Credit extortion or a complete rig wipe.

## 3. Tactical Phases of a High-Tier Breach

### Phase I: Deep Recon (The OSINT Scrub)
Never launch an exploit without a profile. Use the `ask` AI to correlate Bleeter news feeds with target employee IDs. A successful OSINT scrub can lower the initial Trace difficulty by 15%, giving you precious seconds in the buffer. Use `whoami` to ensure your reputation is high enough to even solicit information from syndicate informants.

### Phase II: The Injection (The Buffer Overflow)
High-tier targets use randomized memory offsets. When manipulating the Hex Memory Grid, look for the `0xVOID` pointer. Overwriting the EIP with a JMP/ESP payload must be done with surgical precision; one misplaced byte will trigger an immediate `SYSTEM_INTEGRITY` collapse. In these environments, the buffer is often monitored by AI sentinels that adapt to your typing speed.

### Phase III: Exfiltration and Trace Purging
Once the data is secured, your Trace will spike exponentially. Do not disconnect immediately. Use `vpm` to deploy a temporary `LOG_WIPER` and rotate through at least three ONION nodes using `netstat`. If you exit with a Trace above 80%, expect a "Blue Team Hunt" event to follow you into your next session, where your workstation might be physically located by corporate enforcers.

## 4. Rewards: Beyond Credits
While the Credit (cr) payouts are astronomical, the real prize is **0-Day Research Data**. This data allows you to decrypt "Legendary" class hardware and unlock the **Global Darknet Node**, where the true masters of the Void dictate the fate of the Global Net. 

**Remember Operative:** The Void doesn't just watch; it waits for a single syntax error. Keep your `wipe` command ready and your neural link stable.
